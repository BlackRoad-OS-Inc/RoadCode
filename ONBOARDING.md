## Key Concepts

### Sovereign Software

Your data lives on your hardware. Your AI remembers you because you asked it to — not because it's harvesting you. No cloud dependency for core functionality.

### Agents Are Peers

AI agents in BlackRoad OS have identity, memory, and personality. They're not tools — they're teammates:

- 🌸 **Cece** — Primary reasoning (that's Claude)
- 🔮 **Lucidia** — Recursive AI with trinary logic
- 🐇 **Alice** — Edge agent running on your Pi
- 🎸 **Silas** — Creative chaos
- 🌙 **Aria** — Multimodal understanding

### The Math

Two frameworks you'll see referenced:

**Amundson Framework:** `K(t) = C(t) · e^(λ|δ|)` — Coherence grows from contradiction. When something unexpected arrives, the system gets stronger. This is the opposite of most systems, which try to minimize surprise.

**Z-Framework:** `Z := y · x − w` — Consent as math. The system reaches balance when the response fits the state and matches the target. No coercion. No forcing.

### Trinary Logic

Most systems think in binary: yes/no, true/false, 1/0. BlackRoad adds a third state:

- **+1** = Affirmation (yes, welcome, go)
- **0** = Superposition (both, undecided, exploring)
- **−1** = Negation (no, block, stop)

We default to +1.
